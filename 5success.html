About

Cephtactics is a private dental and orthodontic training and research group based in London. Our specialised software programs in dentistry help the public learn more about dental health in every which way! The Dental Diagnosis program will give some insight in the problems and severity of the symptoms and the importance of getting assistance. Our other aims are to provide training in orthodontics to dentists with interest in short term techniques in the current market. We also endeavour to hone skills of those already practising in both fixed and removable orthodontics.
